Flutter Delivery App
private project

Flutter delivery multi-users app (customer - vendor) with multiple special features: Authentication, Cart, Checkout, Profile, Invoices...etc. So as a customer you can login and search about restaurants, meals, big markets, ..etc. Then choose what you need and add them to the cart. Finally, you will choose delivery time that you want. But as vendor, you can add your own restaurant or market and control it, you can add several products and meals, edit or delete them.

Techniques:

Flutter Framework,Dart Language, Bloc state management, Clean architecture, Restapi, Local storage shared preferences.
