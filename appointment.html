Appointment App
private project

Flutter multi-users app, with different roles : Admin, Customer, Owner and Employee. So you can login as customer to reserve a service or meeting (salon, hospital, restaurant..etc ), or login as owner to add your custom institution, or as employee to see your to-do tasks, or login as admin to manage and see the work log.

Techniques:

Flutter Framework,Dart Language, Provider state management, Restapi, Local storage shared preferences.
