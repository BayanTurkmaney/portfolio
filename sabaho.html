Sabaho App
private project

It is an application that targets individuals with various interests in divination sciences, tarot reading, and palmistry. The paid service application is aimed at the country of Lebanon.

Techniques:

Flutter Framework, Dart language.

Features:

Authentication, discover several foretellers and their services, create requeste order for: (coffee reading, face reading, palm reading, tarot, love status, dream interpretation and ask for help), payment, text messaging and others.
