Tobsir App
private project

It is a multiservice global application that allows you to connect with various clairvoyants around the world and learn about your daily horoscope, palmistry, tarot, and more.

Techniques:

Flutter Framework, Dart language.

Features:

Authentication, video/audio calls, join to live broadcasts, discover several foretellers and their services, create requeste order for: (coffee reading, face reading, palm reading, tarot, love status, dream interpretation and ask for help), payment, text messaging and others.
